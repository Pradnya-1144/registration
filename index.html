ABOUT PHARMA ANVESHAN

Pharma Anveshan is an event organized by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) to celebrate the birth anniversary of Prof. M.L. Schroff. The event also serves as a platform for pharmacy professionals to discuss the National Education Policy, 2020.

Prof. Mahadeva Lal Schroff

Prof. Mahadeva Lal Schroff is regarded as India's founder of pharmacy. He attained this distinction as a result of his accomplishments, which included steering India's pharmaceutical sector and educational system.
